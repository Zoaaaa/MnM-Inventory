# Conversion Tips for Facebook-Based Small Shops in the Philippines

Practical strategies to turn more browsers into buyers using your MnM Cubes catalog.

## 🎯 Core Principle

> The goal is to move customers from "interested" to "ready to order" before they even message you.

Every piece of friction you remove = more sales.

---

## 1. 📱 Optimize for Mobile

- **95%+ of your customers browse on mobile phones** (mostly Android)
- Keep product images clear and fast-loading
- Use large, tappable buttons
- Minimize text — Filipino buyers scan, they don't read paragraphs
- Test your site on a mid-range Android phone (not just your iPhone)

## 2. 💬 Make the Messenger Button Obvious

- Use a bright, contrasting color (the blue Messenger button stands out)
- Place it prominently on every product view
- Prefill the message so customers don't have to type
- Example: *"Hi! I'd like to order: MoYu RS3M 2024. Is this still available?"*
- This saves the customer effort AND gives you structured order info

## 3. 💰 Show Prices Clearly

- **Never hide prices.** "PM for price" loses customers.
- Show prices in PHP with the ₱ symbol
- If you have a sale price, show the original price crossed out
- Round to clean numbers when possible (₱450 not ₱449.99)

## 4. 📦 Show Stock Status

- "In Stock" with a count builds urgency: *"In Stock (3 left)"*
- "Low Stock" creates FOMO (fear of missing out)
- "Out of Stock" prevents wasted messages
- Update stock in real-time after each sale

## 5. 🏷️ Use Clear Categories

- Group products logically (by puzzle type)
- Let customers filter quickly
- Most buyers know what type of cube they want
- Don't make them scroll through everything

## 6. 📸 Product Images Matter

- Use clean, white-background product photos
- Show the cube from a recognizable angle
- Consistent image style builds trust
- Even phone photos work if they're well-lit and consistent

## 7. 🔗 Share Your Catalog Link

Instead of posting individual products on Facebook, share your catalog link:

- **In your Facebook page bio**
- **In Facebook Marketplace listings** — "See all products: [link]"
- **In group posts** — "Full catalog with prices: [link]"
- **In Messenger auto-replies**
- **In your Instagram bio**

This reduces "HM?" messages by 50-80%.

## 8. 💡 Facebook Page Auto-Reply Setup

Set up an auto-reply on your Facebook Page:

> "Thanks for messaging MnM Cubes! 🧊
> 
> Browse our full catalog with live prices and stock here:
> 👉 [your-catalog-url]
> 
> Or tell us what you're looking for and we'll help you out!"

This handles inquiries even when you're asleep.

## 9. 📋 Streamline Your Order Process

When a customer messages to order:
1. Confirm the product and stock
2. Confirm the price
3. Ask for shipping address (or meetup location)
4. Send payment details (GCash, BPI, etc.)
5. Confirm payment received
6. Ship and send tracking number

**Pro tip:** Create saved replies in Messenger for each step.

## 10. 🚚 Shipping & Payment Info

Consider adding a simple info section to your site:
- Accepted payment methods (GCash, Maya, bank transfer)
- Shipping options and estimated costs
- Meetup locations (if applicable)
- Return/exchange policy

## 11. 📊 Track What Sells

Use the admin dashboard to:
- Monitor which products get the most views
- Track low-stock items to reorder in time
- Notice which categories are most popular
- Adjust pricing based on demand

## 12. 🔄 Keep Your Catalog Updated

- Update stock immediately after each sale
- Add new products as soon as they arrive
- Remove discontinued items (or mark as out of stock)
- A stale catalog loses trust quickly

## 13. 🎁 Promotional Ideas

- **Bundle deals:** "3x3 + Lube combo for ₱500"
- **New arrival highlights:** Pin new products at the top
- **Limited stock alerts:** "Only 2 left!" drives urgency
- **Loyalty discounts:** "Returning customer? Message us for a discount code"

## 14. 🌟 Build Trust

- Show real product photos (not just manufacturer images)
- Add a brief "About Us" section
- Display your location (city/province)
- Link to your Facebook page for social proof
- Respond to messages quickly (aim for < 1 hour during business hours)

## 15. 📈 Growth Path

Once your catalog is running smoothly:

1. **Phase 1:** Catalog + Messenger ordering (current)
2. **Phase 2:** Add order request form (collect orders in-app)
3. **Phase 3:** Add GCash/Maya payment links
4. **Phase 4:** Add customer accounts and order history
5. **Phase 5:** Full checkout with shipping integration

Start simple. Scale when demand justifies it.

---

## 🇵🇭 Philippines-Specific Tips

- **GCash is king** — most buyers prefer GCash payment
- **COD builds trust** for first-time buyers (if you can offer it)
- **J&T and Flash Express** are the most common couriers
- **Facebook Groups** are where most cube buyers hang out
- **Payday timing** — post new products around the 15th and 30th
- **Tagalog/Taglish** in your Messenger replies feels more personal
- **"Legit check"** — buyers want to know you're trustworthy. Social proof helps.

---

*These tips are based on common patterns in Philippine Facebook-based small businesses. Adapt them to your specific audience and selling style.*
